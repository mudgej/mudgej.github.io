Nitrox Bank / Compressor Calculator

This calculator is for blending mixes using a low o2% blending gas (fill mix), for example blending using a nitrox compressor, or banked nitrox.

Using this method, the maximum o2% that can be achieved in the final / desired mix is the o2% of the fill mix gas.

As the SSAC nitrox bank is 34%, the primary purpose of this calculator is to work out the quantity of 34% and and air that should be added to acheive a mix other than 34%. However, as the compressor can provide ~38%, other fill mixes can be specified.
