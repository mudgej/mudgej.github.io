Nitrox Bank / Compressor Calculator

Most nitrox calculators assume that the fill mix will always be 100% o2, however this is not the case when using the nitrox compressor or nitrox bank. This calculator is therefore designed to take any percentage of o2 as a fill mix, for example 34% if using the SSAC banked nitrox. This also allows those who are not qualified gas blenders to use the SSAC 34% nitrox bank to obtain fills such as 32%.

Using this method, the maximum FO2 that can be achieved in the final / desired mix is therefore the FO2 of the fill mix gas. Entering a desired mix greater than the fill mix will not stop the calculator from outputting answers, however they of course are not realistic answers as they would require a cylinder to be drained to negative pressure.

In some cases, a cylinder may need to be slightly drained to achieve the desired mix. If the top-off mix to add field below gives a negative result, the cylinder must be drained to the specifed pressure BEFORE filling begins.
